Delete your Rencana account and data

Last updated: April 25, 2026

Aizztech is the developer of the Rencana app listed on Google Play. This page explains how to delete some of your data (and keep your account), or how to delete your whole account, and what happens to your data in each case.

1. Delete some data without deleting your account

Rencana lets you erase your current semester planner data from our servers while keeping your sign-in and profile. Use this if you want a fresh timetable and tasks but not to leave the app.

  1. Open the Rencana app and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Clear data and follow the on-screen steps (including typing the confirmation phrase the app shows). This permanently removes the listed data from your account on our systems.

What is removed in this step: for your account, your timetable, courses, tasks, academic-calendar entries, study-time records, and imported scheme-of-work (SOW) content and files stored for those features.

What is kept: your account (email / sign-in), your profile and university details, and Community data (friends, circles, and related content), unless you delete your entire account (section 2) or remove items in-app where the app offers it.

For other requests (e.g. export) contact support@aizztech.com.

2. Delete your entire account (in the app)

To remove your account and all associated data, use Delete Account in the app. This is separate from Clear data (section 1).

  1. Open the Rencana app on your device and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings (from the app’s main navigation).
  3. Scroll to Delete Account and follow the on-screen instructions. You will be asked to type a confirmation phrase, then to confirm a final time before the account is removed.
Cannot use the app?

Email support@aizztech.com from the email address on your Rencana account, with the subject line “Rencana account deletion request”, and we will process your request after verifying your identity. We may take up to a few business days to complete manual requests.

3. Data that is deleted when you delete your account

When your account is deleted, we remove your personal and app content from our live systems, including (where applicable):

  • Account profile (e.g. name, email, academic profile you entered)
  • Planner data: timetables, tasks, notes, and related content you created
  • Community data: friend links, circle membership, reactions, quick messages, shared tasks, and map-related community preferences
  • Device push tokens and in-app settings tied to the account

4. Data we may keep temporarily

Deletion from primary databases — completed within 30 days of a successful in-app (or verified email) deletion request.

Backups and disaster-recovery copies — encrypted backups may take up to an additional 60 days to be fully purged in our host’s rotation. After that, your data is not used for any purpose.

Legal / compliance — in rare cases we may retain minimal information for as long as required by law, or a short de-identified or aggregated record of the fact that an account previously existed, where applicable (for example, fraud prevention or dispute resolution). We do not use such retention to restore your full account.

5. Subscriptions and stores

If you have an active Google Play or App Store subscription, cancel it in the Play Store or App Store settings. Deleting the Rencana account does not automatically cancel store subscriptions.

6. More detail

For the full list of data categories and your rights, see the Rencana Privacy Policy. Terms of use are also available inside the Rencana app (Settings and legal sections).
